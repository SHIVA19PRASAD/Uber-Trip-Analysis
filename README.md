Project Overview :
Developed an interactive Uber Trip Analysis Dashboard in Power BI to analyze booking trends, revenue generation, trip efficiency, and customer travel behavior for the period of June 2024. The dashboard provides a complete view of Uber trip performance using key operational and financial KPIs, helping businesses make better pricing, service, and customer experience decisions.
The project uses two tables: Uber Trip Details and Location Table, containing trip information such as pickup/drop-off time, passenger count, trip distance, fare amount, surge fee, vehicle type, payment method, and location mapping.

KPI's Used :
Total Bookings , Total Booking Value, Average Booking Value ,Total Trip Distance , Average Trip Distance ,Average Trip Time . These are the KPI's used

Key Insights :
UberX had the highest number of bookings (1,819 trips) and generated the highest revenue ($25,964)
Daytime bookings significantly outperformed night bookings, showing stronger customer demand during working hours
Digital payments dominated over cash transactions, indicating strong customer preference for cashless travel
Short-distance trips formed the majority of bookings, with an average travel distance of just 3 miles
Midtown Center emerged as the highest-demand location, making it a critical operational hotspot

Tools Used : 
Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Data Modeling ,Data Cleaning .

Business Outcome :
Optimizing pricing strategies using booking value trends and mproving vehicle allocation based on demand patterns
Enhancing customer satisfaction through location-based service improvements and supporting business decisions using real-time visual insights .
